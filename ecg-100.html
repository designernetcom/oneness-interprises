ECG 100

Electrocardiograph machines record the electrical activity of the heart. The ECG 100,equipped with Clarity Medical’s proprietary TISA interpretation technology, is a standalone 12-lead ECG machine offering of Clarity Medical. The ECG 100 is available in 4 and 6 channel variants.

4 Channel: Features
  • 4 channel variant
  • All functions accessible through seven button keypad
  • Bright LEDs indicate important functions and settings
  • Equipped with “TISA” – True Interpretation Sensitivity Algorithm
  • Detailed interpretation package
  • Personalized tracings with user’s name (user editable)
  • Excellent printing quality with high resolution printer
  • Quantitative analysis of tracings
  • Simultaneous 12-lead acquisition of patient data
  • Compact and portable
  • Link to PC (optional) and copy facility
  • Simple operation (generation and delivery of report with single key press)
  • Dynamic specification as per user’s preference
6 Channel: Features
  • 6 channel variant
  • All functions accessible through keypad
  • Bright LEDs indicate important functions and settings
  • Equipped with “TISA” – True Interpretation Sensitivity Algorithm
  • Detailed interpretation package
  • Personalized tracings with user’s name (user editable)
  • Excellent printing quality with high resolution printer
