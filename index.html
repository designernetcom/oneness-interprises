What About Us
Oneness Enterprises

Oneness Enterprises is a leading distributor of medical Equipment’s and Surgical Disposable Devices to hospitals, clinics, and healthcare professionals. We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality products and services at competitive prices.
Founded in 2015, our company has grown to become a trusted partner to healthcare professionals across the Maharashtra. We work closely with medical equipment manufacturers and suppliers to ensure that we always have the latest and most advanced medical devices in stock.
Our product portfolio includes a wide range of medical equipment and supplies, including NICU / ICU / OT equipment, Critical Care surgical Disposables. We also provide ongoing maintenance and repair services for all of our products.
